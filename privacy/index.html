Privacy

We respect your privacy. This page summarises how we handle personal information for subscribers and site visitors in Australia.

What we collect

  • Account details: name, email (via Netlify Identity)
  • Billing details: collected and processed by Stripe — we do not store card numbers
  • Content interactions: downloads and basic usage to improve resources

How we use data

  • Provide access to the Library and manage roles (member / subscriber)
  • Send transactional emails (receipts, access updates)
  • Send occasional product updates — you can opt out anytime

Storage & third parties

  • Netlify hosts the site and Identity service
  • Stripe processes payments
  • Mail delivery via our chosen provider for notifications

Cookies

We use essential cookies for login. Optional analytics may set cookies; we keep these minimal.

Your choices

Contact us to access, correct, or delete your data where applicable. You can also delete your account by request.

Questions

Use the contact form and we’ll get back to you.
